Care Guides

Neocaridina Care Guide

  • 🌡️ Temperature: Keep between 18-26°C
  • 💧 Water Parameters: pH 6.5-7.5, TDS 150-250
  • 🍽️ Feeding: Small portions 2-3 times per week
  • 🔄 Water Changes: 10-20% weekly
  • 📝 Regular testing of water parameters

Caridina Care Guide

  • 🌡️ Temperature: Keep between 18–23°C
  • 💧 Water Parameters: pH 5.5-6.5, Use buffering substrate for stable pH. TDS 100–150 ppm
  • 💧 GHeneral Hardness: 4–6 dGH
  • 💧 Carbonate Hardness: 0–2 dKH
  • 🍽️ Feeding: Small portions 2-3 times per week
  • 🔄 Water Changes: 10-20% weekly using remineralized RO water.
  • 📝 Regular testing of water parameters

Plant Care Guide

  • 💡 Lighting: 6-8 hours daily
  • 🌿 Fertilization: Weekly dosing
  • 📏 CO2: Optional but beneficial
  • ✂️ Trimming: As needed for growth
  • 🔍 Monitor for algae growth
